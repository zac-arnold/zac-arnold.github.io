What is your long term goal and or career pathway?

My baseline goal is to complete EDA with the skills needed to find employment as a Junior Web Developer. Having changed career paths I am open to everything, and expect to find my path as I progress through EDA. That being said, I would jump at any opportunity to work with companies in areas like Music, Environmentalism, or Unionism.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

I would like to develop more skills for working in large group environments as well as focus on ways to build concentration.

Describe your own strengths and limitations, and provide commitment to how you will manage the workload in the EDA programme.

I know I work best when I adhere to a routine. With that in mind I start every week by checking how much time each Sprint has been allocated. I then break that down accordingly over five days (knowing that if I get stuck I have Saturday and Sunday as a backup). To keep myself in check throughout the programme I will make sure to stay on task, keep times allocated and take lots of breaks. When self-consciousness rears I will see it for what it is and make sure to dive into that situation.

Provide a commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way.

I will seek help using the Think Like A Programmer model. First analyse the situation, develop a plan, and debug the problem. If I am not successful, I will try another methodology. If that fails I will reach out to other students or my Facilitator via Slack.

Provide a description of what you expect from the facilitation team.

I would expect the facilitation team to be professional, to lead with empathy, understanding and a lot of patience.
